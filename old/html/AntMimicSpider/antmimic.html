What is Encryption?

Encryption is a method of obfuscate file with a secret key and is the most effective way to achieve security.

    Some very popular encryption algorithms

  • AES
  • Twofish
  • Triple DES

What does it protects against?

It prevent someone from getting unauthorized access to your file.

    What it Protect.

  • Crazy Girlfriend
  • Parent
  • Police

    What file should I encryption.

  • Everthing

  • Some important stuff like that no one should look. (´・◡・｀)

How does it work?

The Algorithms will use your secret key plus your data and split out a encrypt data file.

Who needs to use encryption?
